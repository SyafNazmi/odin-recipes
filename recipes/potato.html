Jacket Potato with Beans and Cheese

Description

It's exactly what it says on the tin - a jacket potato (baked potato) with baked beans and grated cheese on top. Yes, it's better than it sounds ðŸ˜‚

Ingredients

Checklists:

  • 2x large Baking Potatoes (approx 350g/12oz EACH)
  • 1x 415g/14oz tin of Baked Beans
  • 100g / 1 cup Cheddar, grated
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • Worcestershire Sauce, as needed (optional)
  • Olive Oil, as needed
  • Salt, as needed

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180C/350F.
  2. Place the potatoes on a baking tray and stab them a few times all over with a fork. Coat in a drizzle of olive oil and a good pinch of salt, then place in the oven for 1 hour 20 mins (should be fork tender all the way through at this point). Crank up the heat to 220C/430F until ultra crispy, then remove.
  3. Just before the end, add the beans to a pot over medium heat and simmer until they're piping hot and the sauce thickens up.
  4. Slice potatoes down the centre and push them open. Add 1 tbsp butter and a good pinch of salt to both. Fluff up with a fork to get the butter & salt in and around the potato.
  5. Top with a pinch of cheese, followed by beans, followed by more cheese then finished with a few dashes of Worcestershire sauce.
